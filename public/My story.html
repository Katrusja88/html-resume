About me

When I moved to Sweden in my 20th, I began to study Swedish and Logistics. Gothenburg (the city where I live) is a port city, and I figured that I would always be employed with this sort of education.

Years went by and in general I enjoyed the world of shipping, but I was longing for tech more and more. I fell in love with UX design when I first came across it and immediately started studying again. After graduation I have worked in several companies (mostly startups) as a UX/UI Designer.

To understand better developers, I decided to take a course in Web Development and this website is a product of the skills I have learnt (and still learning)

On a personal note, my biggest hobby is traveling. There was a year when I visited 10 countries in 10 months! (not bragging!)
